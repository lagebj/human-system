# Seam Observations

**Observational document.** This file tracks where chapter breaks *might* emerge — not where they are decided to be.

Chapter seams are **discovered**, not imposed. They appear where the terrain actually changes, not where topics suggest they should.

---

## How to Use

When reading through the manuscript, note locations where:

- The thinking shifts footing significantly
- A major concept has completed a movement
- The voice or tempo changes naturally
- A new kind of work begins

Record these as observations only. Do not treat them as decisions.

## Principles

- Seams are found where momentum naturally shifts, not where chapters "should" begin
- A seam is valid only if the text on either side feels like different terrain
- Early observations may prove false — confidence grows across passes
- The walk is one continuous thing; seams are conveniences for readers, not truths about the content
