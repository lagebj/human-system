# Alert Systems and Gut Feelings

**Captured:** 2026-06-12

**Source:** Observation during manuscript drafting

**Related to:** Enablement as relocation, competence, alert systems

---

In regards of alert systems, what is actually happening a lot of times is reaction to gut-feelings as well. People learn to spot patterns here. It is not just knowing which alerts to ignore — it is a felt sense of what the alert means in context, built up over time through repeated exposure to the system's behavior.

This gut feeling is a form of competence that lives in the person, not in the system. When you replace the alert system, you are not just changing the signals. You are replacing the ground on which the gut feeling was built.
